Indiana v. Rick Allen

Rick

TL;DR

Rick Allen spent more than 16 years in Delphi, Indiana. Before his 2022 arrest, Rick was known as a devoted husband and father, a friendly face at the town's only pharmacy, and a regular at the local pub. By every measure, he was a stable and remarkably normal guy.

Now serving a 130-year sentence, the trial Court has finally begun to slowly release exhibits that a small group of journalists were only allowed to inspect for 15 minutes at the end of each trial day and with each release, concern grows about the integrity of the investigation and the growing belief that Rick Allen was wrongfully convicted after a tragedy of errors that plague small town police departments.

If you want a to-the-point breakdown of the evidence used against Rick, and juicy issues with the prosecution and trial court you'll want to check out Justice for Rick Allen.

Abby and Libby Reported Missing

The search for the Girls

February 13, 2017 at 5:20 pm

High Bridge Trail

  • After social media posts from family asking anyone who's seen the girls make the rounds to no avail, Mike Patty reports the girls missing.
  • After the family is interviewed at the Sheriff's station, law enforcement asks the family to retrieve any/all devices belonging to the girls to begin searching for information about the girl's comms and movements leading up to their disappearance.
  • The official search was called off around midnight and the Sheriff tells the public there won't be an Amber Alert as they have no information that the girls are in any danger.
  • Hundreds of volunteers show up the next morning to help search and the girls' bodies are found at 12:15 pm on February 15th, 2017.
  • Police announce they are investigating this as a double homicide murder.

The Manhunt for Bridge Guy

Police identify Bridge Guy as a Suspect

February 19, 2017

Delphi, IN

  • After a week of being unable to identify Bridge Guy, police announce he is no longer a person of interest and confirm Bridge Guys is their main suspect.
  • On February 22nd, police hold a press conference revealing the image of the suspect was taken from a video captured by Libby and they release an audio excerpt of an unknown male heard in the background saying "Down the hill."

    Dan Dulin's Investigation

    September 21, 2022

    Who Knows

    • After he was contacted by Liggett and Mullin and not being able to find any audio for his interview with Rick Allen, Dulin decided to look Rick up in his DNR database and sees Rick's fishing license renewal April 2017 showed a 2 inch height change.
    • Despite the fact that Dulin didn't think Rick Allen purportedly saying he was at the trail from 1:30 to 3:30 pm the day the girls went missing was significant enough to contact Liggett and Mullin, Dulin felt this change on Rick's fishing license of 2 inches — closer to Bridge Guy's height — could be a significant indicator that Rick Allen took steps after the murder to look less like Bridge Guy.
    • Dulin immediately reported this bombshell lead to Liggett and Mullin.
