    A little more info:

    After the 3-day hearings last summer, I found out a small number of people had been taking on the costs of all the pre-trial hearing transcripts to make available to the public so I offered to do a trivia night fundraiser stream to help crowdsource the funds. However, prior to trial, Jodie Williams (Judge Gull's Court Reporter) informed Criminality that there would be a significant delay in completing the remaining transcripts for the 3-day hearings and an even longer delay in completing the trial transcripts, so the trivia night fundraiser plans were postponed.

    Since that time, Criminality has been in touch with the Court off and on and recently followed up with Jodie who provided a quote of $4,600 for all remaining transcripts. Jodie has until July 9th, 2025 to complete the review of the record for Richard Allen's appellate lawyers and we expect the transcripts will be sent shortly after that task is complete.

    My schedule is a lot different now than what it was when I offered to do the trivia night fundraiser stream so I've started sharing Criminality's links for people who want to pitch in just in case scheduling efforts cut too close to July 9th bc I don't want Criminality to end up fronting the bulk of the costs for the money order that will need to be provided to the Court — especially considering Criminality is going to make the transcripts freely available and accessible, without watermarks, to the public.

    Teamwork Makes The Dream Work

    We've already reached half our goal and if we meet the full goal before I can arrange the fundraiser stream, we can do a celebration stream instead! Thank you SO much to everyone who's pitched in so far!!
