Examinations

Four 'examinations' of firearms were conducted during the duration of the Delphi Murders Investigation— three in 2017 and one in 2022. Unspent rounds were cycled through suspect guns and compared against an unfired .40 S&W cartridge with extraction markings found in the ground at the crime scene.

During the fourth examination, this lady fired a bullet after her first four attempts to cycle a test round through Richard Allen's gun failed to produce a match. She still had to use research for different guns to suppplement her findings because, even after firing the damn thing, it still produced an insufficient agreement of anything. She was ultimately unable to exclude any of the firearms she examined. Smdh.
