Four 'examinations' of firearms were conducted during the duration of the Delphi Murders Investigation. Unspent rounds were cycled through suspect guns and compared against an unfired .40 S&W cartridge with extraction markings found in the ground at the crime scene.

This lady fired a bullet when her first four attempts to cycle the round through Richard Allen's gun failed to produce a match. She still had to use research for different guns to suppplement her findings because, even after firing the damn thing, it still produced an insufficient agreement of anything. She was ultimately unable to exclude *any* of the firearms she examined. Smdh.
