Examinations

Four firearms 'examinations' of firearms were conducted throughout the duration of the Delphi Murders Investigation—three in 2017 and one in 2022. Unspent rounds were cycled through suspect guns and compared against an unfired .40 S&W cartridge with extraction markings found in the ground at the crime scene.
