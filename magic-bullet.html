The Magic Bullet

Examinations

Four known 'examinations' of firearms were conducted throughout the duration of the Delphi Murders Investigation—two in 2017 and two in 2022. Unspent rounds were cycled through suspect guns and compared against an unfired .40 S&W cartridge with extraction markings found in the ground at the crime scene. No clue where the State's reports for Brad Weber and Rawn Logan's guns are.
