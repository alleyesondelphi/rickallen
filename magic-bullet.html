Examinations

Four 'examinations' of firearms were conducted throughout the duration of the Delphi Murders Investigation—three in 2017 and one in 2022. Unspent rounds were cycled through suspect guns and compared against an unfired .40 S&W cartridge with extraction markings found in the ground at the crime scene.

Submission 1 — Request 0003

February 17, 2017

Glock Model 22, 40 S&W caliber pistol belonging to Jim Maxwell (serial number NR668 US) and a magazine containing 15 Federal 40 S&W caliber cartridges.
