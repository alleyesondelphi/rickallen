    2. First 48 hours timeline
    • 8:30 am: Theresa and Dave Liebert were just back from vacation and home in Delphi as of February 12, 2017. They went tanning at 8:30 am on February 13, 2017. Theresa said on their way home, they saw a guy with no teeth who was wearing all black. She said he turned around when they saw him. Theresa saw him walking toward the green gate by their house but as they approached, he stopped and started walking the opposite way. He was dressed for cold weather even tho it wasn't cold out. She described a white scruffy beard, a big hat — maybe a knit cap — and a scarf across his chin. He grinned at Theresa and she thinks she saw silver caps on his teeth. He also had high cheekbones.
    • (Testimony of Theresa Liebert on Oct 31, 2024) (Theresa Liebert Affidavit re: suspect at 8:30 am on February 13, 2017) (Theresa Liebert Sketch) (GYTB, 1:46:20 and 2:07:40 mark)
    • 8:45 am: Brad Heath drives by an older model vehicle parked on the side of the road halfway between the CPS building and entrance to FB on his way to work. He described it as an “older vehicle” that was 4 doors and a faded, dark blue color. He estimated the vehicle was a late 80s or early 90s model.
    • (Testimony of Brad Heath on Nov 1, 2024) (BBB — photo of vehicle resembling the one Brad Health saw around 2:06 pm) (Delphi - Another Vehicle? Interview from 2021, 0:40 mark)
    • Approx 9:00 am to 10:00 am: “Abby and Libby woke that Monday around 10 a.m. after staying up late the night before. Libby Snapchats with her mom, Carrie Timmons ❤️. Derek made them banana pancakes for breakfast.” Abby and Libby “were piddling around, and she was bored, so they came out and asked if we could do something, and I had to work the rest of the day. So I told her, I said, well, you know what, I've got some filing that needs to be done. If you guys will do that filing for me, I'll pay you for it, and then later on, after we get off, after I get done working, we'll go shopping or go do something, because Libby had started enjoying shopping.”
    • (Scene of the Crime Ep: 1, 20:49 and 22: 48 mark)
    • 10:30 am: Elvis Fields sets his phone down in Rushville and doesn't touch it whatsoever for the next roughly 9 hours.
    • (Supplemental Report #542 re: Joyce Moffitt interview on August 22, 2018) (Murphy Report p. 1) (Elvis February 27, 2018 interview at the 14:02:30 mark) (Franks I p. 96-97, footnotes 129-130)
